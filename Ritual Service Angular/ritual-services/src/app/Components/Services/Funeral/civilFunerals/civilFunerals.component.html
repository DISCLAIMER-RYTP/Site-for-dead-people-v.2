Civil Funeral

Civil funerals are funerals without religious rites. Strictly speaking, this is one of the types of secular mourning ceremony, the script and conduct of which is entrusted to a highly qualified presenter - the master of ceremonies. In addition to the traditional scenario, this event can reflect all the wishes of the relatives of the deceased. As a rule, it is a personal farewell to the deceased, commemoration of his achievements and life.

Farewell to the deceased (the so-called civil memorial service) can be organized in the farewell hall at the morgue, directly in the crematorium or in the cemetery. To organize a civil memorial service at the place of work of the deceased KP, the Ritual Service provides the services of a master of ceremonies, as well as organizes the rental of ritual accessories.

Civil funerals involve burial in the ground or cremation.
