Elite Funeral

IP funerals are special funerals for the deceased, who had a high social status during his lifetime. What does it mean? People who by the will of their efforts, origins and achievements have become the elite of society. Relatives and friends of the deceased seek to find a ritual agency, whose staff organizes funerals at the highest level. Most often, not only close people come to the funeral of VIPs, but also the press and colleagues, who also have a high social status.

VIP-funeral - an elite service

The main difference between VIP and ordinary funerals is the presence of a clear plan of events and control over every minute of the mourning event. Everything from the choice of funeral paraphernalia to the organization of a funeral dinner is organized at the highest level.

The main advantage of such a funeral - there is no need for constant presence of the customer. High-class ritual agencies, organizing VIP-funerals, take all the worries and worries. Relatives who have suffered a loss can avoid the exhausting process of choosing the necessary for the funeral, they do not worry that something will go wrong. Also, the employee of the funeral agency will take care of the paperwork, if necessary - will provide a meeting of the body of the deceased at the airport or special vehicles.

There are several types of VIP funerals:

  • When a funeral is arranged for a famous deceased - a film actor, journalist, writer, cultural figure or politician. In this case, the organization focuses on the presence of a large number of people - the press, fans, relatives and friends. At such funerals it is necessary to organize and ensure an appropriate level of order and protection.
  • For people who do not want publicity and the presence of outsiders, the staff of the funeral agency can organize a chamber funeral. Usually such a VIP service is ordered for the funeral of rich people, such as successful businessmen. In this case, the staff of the funeral agency will also take on all the worries associated with the organization. The information received during their work will remain confidential and the funeral will be held at the highest level.

The work of a ritual agent during the preparation and organization of a VIP funeral

The duties of a ritual VIP-agent include the following:

  • Supervision of the work of all those involved in the organization of the funeral;
  • If necessary - writing a script, choosing a farewell room;
  • Organization of farewell within the walls of the institution - the place of work of the deceased (design of the farewell hall);
  • Provision of elite transport and accompanying vehicles;
  • Organization of a funeral dinner, burial, according to the religious beliefs of the deceased.
  • What VIP items and accessories are needed for a funeral of this level?

When organizing an elite funeral, ritual goods and accessories of the highest price category are traditionally used. Everything from the coffin to the mourning wreaths and ribbons must correspond to the high social status of the deceased and show respect for his merits.

VIP-transport and support crews

Employees of the funeral agency will help to choose a ritual VIP-transport - hearse and cars for accompanying persons, relatives and loved ones of the deceased.

During the VIP-funeral, the employees of the funeral agency are obliged to ensure the presence of accompanying vehicles. This is, first of all, the traffic police, which will provide unimpeded and quick access to the cemetery, as well as ambulances in case any of the relatives, friends, friends of the deceased become ill.
