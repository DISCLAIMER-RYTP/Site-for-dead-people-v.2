Preparation Deceased

  • Delivery of the deceased to the morgue
  • Body washing
  • Tamponing
  • Embalming the deceased;
  • Dressing the deceased
  • Makeup
  • Sanitary treatment of the premises
  • Disposal of household items

First of all, we deliver the body of the deceased to the morgue, where we perform all necessary procedures.

Our employees wash their bodies directly in the morgue in preparation for the funeral. We use special disinfectant aromatic and antibacterial substances. Due to this, we hide the decomposition processes while the farewell processes are taking place. Equally important is the issue of tamponade - a procedure for installing special tampons impregnated with drugs to prevent unwanted discharge during the funeral. Embalming will allow you to maintain a normal skin color and prevent the formation of specific odors if mourning events are delayed. In general, it is enough to treat the body with thanatogel after washing. Dressing the deceased is carried out in accordance with the canons and traditions of the religion to which the deceased belonged. Clothes are provided by relatives, but if you need a consultation - we are here. Make-up is a continuation of a set of procedures to ensure that the face and body of the deceased do not show signs of decomposition and he looked natural - as he was remembered by relatives and friends for life. Cosmetics are applied to the face and exposed parts of the body - the hands. In addition to the above procedures, which are directly related to the preparation of the body for burial, we also perform disinfection of the premises of the deceased, as well as dispose of household items. Note that these actions are a normal civilized practice and should not be inconvenient for you.

IMPORTANT MOMENT. In case of need of postponement of burial we provide individual refrigerating chambers for a body.

After all the activities, the deceased was prepared for burial. Next is the farewell ceremony. We provide such a service. For details, you can use the menu item "Services", where you can see the entire list of our services.
