The death occurred at night

  • Call an ambulance, which states the death;
  • Call police officers, who will draw up a report on the examination of the corpse;
  • Be sure to receive a death certificate and a protocol of examination of the corpse from medical staff and police, respectively;
  • If necessary, call a special team to transport the deceased to the morgue (phone numbers can be found in the ambulance crew). The report of the examination of the corpse and the death certificate must be handed over to the officers of the summoned service for provision to the morgue together with the body. At the same time it is necessary to receive from employees of service on transportation of the dead the form of the direction to polyclinic (to issue the ambulatory card if it is not present on hands);
  • If necessary, call a special team to transport the deceased to the morgue (phone numbers can be found in the ambulance crew). The report of the examination of the corpse and the death certificate must be handed over to the officers of the summoned service for provision to the morgue together with the body. At the same time it is necessary to receive from employees of service on transportation of the dead the form of the direction to polyclinic (to issue the ambulatory card if it is not present on hands);
  • If the body was not transported to the morgue, then in the morning you should go to the clinic to obtain a medical certificate of death. You can apply to the district doctor or the head of the polyclinic on this issue, and you must have a corpse examination report, a death certificate, a medical policy, as well as an outpatient card of the deceased;
  • If the body was transported to the morgue, then in the morning you should go to the clinic to obtain an outpatient card of the deceased with a record of the posthumous epicrisis. It is also necessary to have a referral from the deceased's transport service, a medical policy, the deceased's passport and the applicant's passport. After receiving an outpatient card (with mandatory post-mortem epicrisis), you should apply to the morgue for further registration of a medical death certificate;
  • With a death certificate obtained at the polyclinic (or morgue) you need to apply to the registry office (at the place of residence, or to the next registry office during non-working days, or at the location of the clinic / morgue) to issue a death certificate and a stamp death certificate. ;
  • Contact the Ritual Service in Rivne to organize a funeral.

Death came not at home (outside the city, visiting)

  • Call an ambulance and police from the area where the death occurred. Obtain a death certificate from ambulance workers. Receive from police officers the protocol of examination of the corpse and registration of the referral of the deceased for forensic autopsy;
  • Call a special service to transport the deceased to the morgue of the area where the death occurred. All the documents listed above are handed over to the staff of the called service for submission to the morgue together with the body;
  • Next, you should go to the morgue, where the deceased was taken, to obtain a medical death certificate. Carry the passport of the deceased and the passport of the applicant;
  • With the received medical certificate of death to address in РАЦС for reception of the certificate of death of the established sample and the stamp certificate of death. In the absence of a stamp certificate of death, it is forbidden to transport the body to another morgue !;
  • Contact the Ritual Service in Kropyvnytskyi to organize a funeral.

The death occurred during the day

  • Call a district doctor, who will examine the body and state the death, as well as draw up a form stating the death;
  • Call the police, whose staff will provide a report on the examination of the corpse;
  • Having a death certificate, a corpse examination report, a medical policy and an outpatient card of the deceased (if available), the passport of the deceased and the applicant's passport, you should contact the clinic to obtain a medical death certificate, or to resolve the need for referral. deceased for autopsy;
  • If a decision is made on the need for an autopsy, call a special team to transport the dead to the morgue. Then you need to go to the morgue, where the body was taken, to obtain a medical certificate of death. Carry the passport of the deceased and the passport of the applicant;
  • To receive a death certificate of the established sample in the Registry Office, as well as a stamp death certificate. To do this, you must provide the registry office with a medical death certificate;
  • Contact the Ritual Service in Kirovohrad to organize a funeral.

Preparation of documents

During the funeral, the registration of documents after death is one of the most important periods. For a fast procedure of registration of documents it is necessary to study all necessary list of documents. Registration of documents after death is carried out in the relevant institutions. This procedure takes place only upon presentation of the passport of the person responsible for organizing the funeral. The applicant may be a relative of the deceased or another citizen who has undertaken to conduct and organize the funeral.

Each document issued by a medical organization or a registry office requires careful verification of the records entered in it. Particular attention should be paid to the dates, the accuracy of writing by officials of the column indicating the surname, patronymic of the deceased, the date of his birth and death, place of birth and death, as well as a clear indication of the cause of death. It is necessary to make sure that the papers are certified by the seal of the organization and signed by officials.

  • Death statement form (issued by the ambulance crew);
  • Medical death certificate (issued by a clinic doctor or morgue staff);
  • Corpse examination report (issued by a police officer);
  • Stamp certificate of death of the registry office (issued by the registry office);
  • Death certificate (form 33);
  • Certificate for receiving state assistance for burial (issued by employees of the registry office);
  • Agreement on the provision of ritual services (executed by the agent of the funeral service).

He died in a hospital

  • The attending physician or nurse notifies the relatives of the death and informs how to find out the information in which morgue the deceased is and how to obtain a death certificate. As a rule, the body of the deceased is in the morgue of the medical institution and, at the request of relatives, may be there until the moment of transportation of the deceased to another morgue or burial place;
  • With the received death certificate it is necessary to apply to the Registry Office for registration of the death certificate of the established sample and the stamp death certificate;
  • Contact the Ritual Service in Kirovohrad to organize a funeral.

    • Important to know:

  • A medical certificate of death is issued by the staff of the polyclinic only if no more than 10 working days have passed since the last visit of the deceased to the doctor;
  • Carefully check all the information provided in the medical certificate of death, as it is prohibited by law to make any corrections to this document.

Preparing things for the morgue

It is very important to escort the deceased to the last mourning path with his worthy honors. The loss is irreparable, the grief of relatives and friends is inconsolable, but it is simply necessary to keep the image of the deceased in one's memory. Ritual clothing is of great importance in preserving and fixing the positive image of the deceased.

    For a man:
  • underwear;
  • socks;
  • suit, shirt, tie;
  • shoes (or slippers);
  • shaving machine, eau de toilette (cologne);
  • toilet soap, comb, terry towel (small).
    • For women:
  • underwear;
  • stockings (or tights);
  • long-sleeved dress (suit or bathrobe);
  • headscarf (not black);
  • shoes (or slippers);
  • toilet water, soap, comb, towel.

  • Get a medical certificate of death in the morgue;
  • In the Registry Office to issue a stamp certificate of death;
  • Contact the Ritual Service in Kirovohrad to organize a funeral.

Death came abroad

In many foreign countries, local law requires that a death application be filed with the local registry office. In any case, it is recommended to additionally make an application to the consulate of the country of which the deceased had citizenship, and ask the consular services to register the fact of death in their register. Completion of these formalities in the future will allow to obtain a death certificate in the country of permanent residence of the deceased.
